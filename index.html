What is SelfBio?

SelfBio is a simple, open-source profile page for everyone. One page that represents you — your links, your identity, your presence.

Built on GitHub Pages, fully customizable, and completely free.

How to claim your username

  1. Join our Discord.
  2. Create a ticket in the category "selfbio claim"
  3. Enter your username, avatar, and style. Once everything has been confirmed, you should receive a message that it's ready.
  4. In principle, you can have any name. Unless it's a rare one, like a 1 to 3-letter name, sometimes even up to 4 letters, these can cost between €5 and €10.
  5. If you're interested, join our Discord below.
